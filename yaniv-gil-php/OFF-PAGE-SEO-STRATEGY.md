## Understanding These Metrics

### Domain Authority (DA) - Currently: 5
- **What it is:** Moz's prediction of how well a site will rank (0-100 scale)
- **Why it's low:** New domains start low; takes 6-12+ months to build
- **Goal:** Reach DA 20-30 in 6-12 months

### Trust Flow - Currently: 0
- **What it is:** Majestic SEO metric measuring quality of backlinks
- **Why it's 0:** No quality backlinks detected yet
- **Goal:** Build links from trusted legal/Israeli websites

### Citation Flow - Currently: 5
- **What it is:** Number of links pointing to your site
- **Why it's low:** Only 9 links detected (Moz Links)
- **Goal:** Increase to 50-100+ quality links

- Focus on relevance: Legal sites, Israeli business directories
- Natural growth is better than rapid artificial growth

## Why Your Technical SEO Score is High But Off-Page is Low

**This is completely normal!** Here's why:

1. **Technical SEO** = What you control (meta tags, structure, speed)
   - ✅ You've done this perfectly
   
2. **Off-Page SEO** = What others do (backlinks, mentions)
   - ❌ This takes time and external factors
   - ⏳ You can influence but not fully control this

**Think of it like:**
- **Technical SEO** = Building a great store
- **Off-Page SEO** = Getting people to talk about your store

You've built a great store, now you need to get it noticed!
